Volunteers

RoboTeam WIE: Aprendiendo a Programar Junto

Technical mentor since March 2018. We encourage more boys and girls (between 8 and 10 years old or 15 and 18 years old, depending on the course) to become interested in STEM fields, promoting diversity and teamwork.
Official website: https://ewh.ieee.org/r9/argentina/roboteam/index.html

Robotito WIE: Robótica para Chicos y Chicas

Technical mentor between 2016 and 2017. We encouraged more boys and girls between 8 and 10 years old to become interested in STEM fields, promoting diversity and teamwork.

Arduino Courses: Introducción de Jóvenes y Adultos a las Nuevas Tecnologías

Technical mentor for five Arduino courses directed at youth, students, faculty, and staff, both within and outside ITBA. A highly notable course was designed for high school teachers and funded by the Buenos Aires City Government (“Course in New Technologies”), which also featured topics on renewable energy and 3D printing.

Nodo Comunitario

A project carried out between 2011 and 2012, in which we repaired obsolete computers from the University of Buenos Aires (UBA) and distributed them to underprivileged schools and institutions, or to projects such as Educ.ar.

LaTeX Seminar - Speaker

In March 2016 and September 2017, I conducted introductory LaTeX courses under the auspices of the IEEE Argentine Joint Chapter and ITBA.
