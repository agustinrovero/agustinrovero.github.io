CAN Bus Low Speed Module

This PCB implements a low speed CAN bus transceiver "shield" (or interchangable module) based on the TJA1055T/3 IC from NXP. It contains all necessary components in a 1350x850 mils board with a M2 screw hole for secure fastening.
The design is breadboard-friendly and follows the guidance in the AN0801 application note.
